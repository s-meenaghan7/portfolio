Intro

Beginning my endeavor in March 2019, I enrolled in a Bachelor of Science in Software Development degree program with Western Governors University. My coding experience is primarily in Java and focused on applying what I've learned about object-oriented programming and design, but I also am competent with languages like Python, SQL, C++, and HTML5/CSS3.

I am currently seeking employment (or internship) with an organization as an associate, apprentice, new grad or junior level software engineer (in-office or remote). Although I currently am most proficient with Java and the backend of development, I am open to any position and confident in my abilities to learn new technologies or languages quickly and adapt to change rapidly.
